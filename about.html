Our Mission

Notre mission

XRPL Canada is a non-profit community organization dedicated to promoting and expanding the XRP Ledger ecosystem across Canada. We support developers, entrepreneurs, and businesses in building innovative solutions on the XRP Ledger.

XRPL Canada est une organisation communautaire à but non lucratif dédiée à l'expansion de l'écosystème XRP Ledger à travers le Canada.

Our mission is to serve as the central hub for XRP Ledger innovation in Canada, providing education, resources, and community support to help build the future of value transfer and blockchain technology.

Notre mission est de servir de centre principal pour l'innovation XRP Ledger au Canada.

What is the XRP Ledger?

Qu'est-ce que le XRP Ledger?
